About Me

Hi! I’m Jonny Shen, finishing up my bachelor’s in Electrical Engineering at UC Davis. I originally started in Computer Science and Engineering, but I didn’t find much enjoyment in coding by itself. Since I’ve always loved working hands‑on with PC parts and building things, switching to EE made the most sense for me.

I was born in Los Angeles and moved to North Carolina as a kid, growing up in a small neighborhood in Chapel Hill. I really wanted to broaden my horizons and try something new, so I set my sights on schools like UC Davis, and here I am!
